## Independent implementation judgment

The product owner authorized one independent implementation judge after CP7 publication and green hosted CI. The judge reviewed exact published commit `a67538edd9f1df91e4790e6795c1b16ca6e3ce2f` read-only, independently reproduced the clean archive and complete checks, inspected the compiled Electron empty state, and returned **FAIL**.

Three blocking findings require correction:

1. the bounded-process promise resolves before the killed subprocess emits `close`;
2. cancel-before-worker-configuration can still send the cancelled probe, retain the pending client job, and make immediate replacement return `INTERNAL_ERROR`;
3. the renderer omits real/base frame rate, time base, rotation, and warning codes required by the frozen ready summary.

The full evidence, nonblocking findings, retries, and readiness decision are recorded in `docs/specs/M1.0-independent-judge-report.md`.

### Judge correction status

The product owner subsequently authorized a narrow correction. The local implementation now:

- latches cancellation/timeout/output-limit before termination but completes the bounded process only after child `close`;
- settles and suppresses unsent cancelled probes, queues immediate replacement behind already-sent cancellation cleanup, and makes a late no-active-job cancel idempotent;
- renders real/base frame rate, time base, rotation, audio details, and the exact closed warning codes;
- removes cancellation-only outcomes from the renderer error-code schema and records operation-specific retry ownership;
- ignores the privacy-approved local `videos-teste/` directory while keeping the binary/LFS guard effective for tracked and nonignored untracked files.

The test-first mapping, focused red/green evidence, private-input policy, publication result, failed hosted CI, and authorized follow-up are recorded in `docs/specs/M1.0-implementation-judge-correction.md`.
